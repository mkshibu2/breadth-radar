07 📖 Indicator Definitions
Breadth Score
Weighted composite: A/D ratio (35%), % above EMA20 (20%), % above SMA50 (15%), % above SMA200 (15%), 52W H/L ratio (15%). Scored 0–100.
Breadth Momentum
Day-over-day change in Breadth Score. Positive = participation improving. A jump of +10 in one day signals a strong breadth expansion event.
Breadth Thrust
3-day change in Breadth Score. Above +10 signals rapid expansion in market participation — an early indicator of potential sustained rally conditions.
Distribution Days
Days where Nifty 50 closed lower on higher volume — institutional selling. 6+ in 25 sessions = elevated risk. Auto-counted from the Pressure Day Log.
Market Pressure (MPM)
Accumulation days minus Distribution days over last 25 sessions. Positive = institutions buying more than selling. Negative = distribution dominating.
BSR — Breakout Success Rate
% of your logged breakout trades that gained 5%+ from pivot. Tells you whether the current market environment is rewarding breakout entries.
FTD — Follow-Through Day
A gain of 1.7%+ on above-average volume on Day 4 or later of a rally attempt. Per O'Neil, this is the single most reliable confirmation of a new market uptrend.
Rally Probability Gauge
Combines Breadth Score, Momentum, Thrust, BSR, Distribution Days, and Market Pressure into a 0–100% probability of a sustained rally. FTD adds a +10 confirmation bonus. Shown as a purple arc gauge on the analysis strip.
